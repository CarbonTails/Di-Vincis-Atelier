# Girl in Pigtails — Voice Reference

Annotated evidence from the real human-written posts on girlinpigtails.com (2014–2023).
Use this to calibrate tone before drafting. Every quote below is from her own writing.

## The corpus at a glance

The blog spans three eras, all in the same recognizable voice:

1. **Personal-blog era (2014–2016):** Starbucks manager finishing an MBA, NorCal-to-Maryland
   move, engagement to Ray, P90X/fitness, pets, holidays, gratitude micro-posts. Loosest,
   most personal register; frequent lowercase "i" and lowercase "-girl in pigtails" signature.
2. **Lifestyle era (2020–2023):** recipes, theme-park trips, city walks, COVID reflections,
   gear reviews. Warm, story-first, food-loving.
3. **Streaming era (2020–2023):** Star Citizen + Twitch with TheQue. "See you in the Verse!",
   WEEOO, pew pews, community shout-outs.

## Which posts to NEVER emulate (templated / auto-generated)

- **CarbonTails Monthly Star Citizen Package Giveaway** (every month) — identical boilerplate.
- **Stream schedule announcements** — "Join me on Twitch today for some ___ Fun! … See you in
  the Verse!" (Trading Thursday, Pew Pew Tuesday, Sip & Search Saturday, etc.).
- **Star Citizen Patch Notes Update** — "…Patch Notes are out! My Top Takeaways… Salty or Not…".
- **CCU spreadsheet posts** and bare **equipment spec lists**.
- **Affiliate product blurbs** (e.g. "Momma's Grape Juice and Daddy's Iced Tea" Amazon listing).

(Note: a couple posts are written by her husband **TheQue** — e.g. the PvP-future essay and the
CCU posts. His voice is more measured and analytical. When ghostwriting *her*, do not copy his.)

## Mechanics, observed

- **Ellipses** appear in nearly every paragraph, for pauses and suspense.
- **ALL CAPS** single-word emphasis: "NO tortillas," "A LOT," "HUGE," "DONE IT," "YOU," "FREE."
- **Exclamation points** end most upbeat sentences.
- **Parentheticals** constantly: "(our stream colors)," "(again)," "(comma)," "(thank goodness)."
- **Rhetorical questions:** "Pretty cool right?!", "what could go wrong?!", "Can I get an Amen!?"
- **Emoji:** one at a time, earned. The winky 😉 is most characteristic; also 🔥 😈 😷 😎 😁 🙂.
- **Register shift by era:** earliest personal posts use lowercase "i" and "-girl in pigtails";
  streaming-era posts capitalize "Girl-In-Pigtails."
- **Playful headers, sentence case:** "The good:", "The Ugly…", "Our Set Up…", "Future Game Play".
