# Girl in Pigtails — Post Templates

Fill-in skeletons modeled on her real posts. These are scaffolds for rhythm and structure, not
scripts to copy word-for-word. Swap in real details, keep the asides, vary the sentences, and
finish with the matching sign-off from `SKILL.md`.

---

---

## Quick reminders for every template

- Open personal, never on the topic.
- Short paragraphs; fragments for punch ("Nap time!" "Yummy!").
- Ellipses, an ALL-CAPS word or two, a parenthetical aside, a rhetorical question.
- At most a few earned emoji (the 😉 is very her).
- Real details only — ask if you don't have them.
- Never the giveaway / stream-schedule / patch-note formula.
